About Me

I am a Senior studying Computer Science at Stony Brook University. Expected to graduate Spring 2021 and currently hacking away on Leetcode

I've had a passion for building and creating ever since I was young. It began with Legos and swiftly moved to Robotics once I got my hands on motors. I thought I set on pursuing engineering but one day, my dad brought home a computer. I quickly discovered the how programs work and the scability of said programs I was in awe by the flexibility and versatility of the computer and the seemingly neverending rabbit hole of math computuation.

My most recent project features a pathfinding visualizer, showcasing popular pathfinding algorithms such as Dijkstra’s and A* algorithm. I also implemented Best-First Search, Bidirectional Search, BFS, DFS to highlight the significance of efficient pathfinding algorithms. And to supplement all these pathfinding algorithms, I implemented maze generation functionality using recursive division, vertical division and horizon division. I hope you enjoy playing around with my project as much as I did making it.
