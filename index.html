About Me

I am a senior studying Computer Science at Stony Brook University. Expected to graduate Spring 2021. Currently hacking away on Leetcode.

I've had a passion for building and creating ever since I was young. It began with Legos and swiftly moved to Robotics once I got my hands on motors. I thought I was set on being an engineer but one day, my dad brought home a computer. I quickly discovered programs and their flexibility to solve any problem. Combined with almost infinite scalability, programs were a better form of Robotics. That was the momeent I knew I wanted to become a Software Engineer.

My most recent project is a pathfinding visualizer, showcasing opular Pathfinding Algorithms such as Dijkstra’s and A* algorithm. I also implemented Best-First Search, Bidirectional Search, BFS, and DFS to highlight the significance of efficient pathfinding algorithms. To supplement all these pathfinding algorithms, I implemented maze generation functionality using recursive division, vertical division and horizon division. I hope you have as much fun playing around with my project as I did making it.
